Ameelio

Ameelio believes in long-established research—increased contact between inmates and loved ones significantly reduces recidivism and improves lives.

See our code

Problem

Communication methods and platforms can be difficult or costly to access in prisons nowadays.

Solution

By building a free video visitation and e-messaging platform, we can provide more connections between the incarcerated and their family members, thus fundamentally improving the lives of everyone touched by incarceration.
